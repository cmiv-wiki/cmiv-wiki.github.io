    This number leads to Stöd och Service, which is the central help function at the hospital.
  • Modality managers
    Each scanner has modality manager which have an overall responsible and knowledge about the system and should be notified if something is not working.

How do I join the CMIV teams group?

Everyone using the cameras at CMIV should be in the corresponding CMIV teams group, as this is the forum for announcing important information regarding the scanner. Any user can also use it to ask for help and share knowledge with other users.

To join contact Maria Kvist

When does a nurse need to be present?

CMIV rules say that for all scans with participants who are considered patients, a research nurse/physician/other healthcare provider has to be present during the whole time. For healthy volunteers this is not a requirement. Regardless, you are responsible for safety and conducting the scan. The nurse is only there for:

  1. Medical supervision.
  2. Administration in RIS and PACS.

For more information see MR guidelines.

When is a "Metodbok" necessary?

You are required to create a "Metodbok" (method book) if you are scanning participants in your study with the help of clinical staff, who will be using the book to assure that the correct scanning procedure is being followed.

To create a metodbok you need to consult the clinical personal in your project in order to setup the necessary documentation.
